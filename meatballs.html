Swedish Meatballs Recipe

Description

Swedish meatballs are a traditional Swedish dish consisting of seasoned beef and pork meatballs in a rich, creamy gravy. They are typically served over egg noodles or mashed potatoes and are a popular comfort food in Sweden. This recipe is easy to make and is sure to be a hit with your family and friends!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley, for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a small bowl, combine breadcrumbs and milk. Let sit for 5 minutes.
  3. In a large bowl, mix together onion, parsley, salt, pepper, allspice, nutmeg, and ginger.
  4. Add ground beef, ground pork, egg, and breadcrumb mixture to the bowl. Mix until well combined.
  5. Shape mixture into 1-inch meatballs and place on a baking sheet.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until meatballs are cooked through.
  7. In a large skillet, melt butter over medium heat.
  8. Whisk in flour and cook for 1-2 minutes, until golden brown.
  9. Gradually whisk in beef broth and heavy cream. Cook, stirring constantly, until mixture thickens.
  10. Season with salt and pepper, to taste.
  11. Add meatballs to the skillet and simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  12. Serve meatballs over egg noodles or mashed potatoes, garnished with chopped parsley.
